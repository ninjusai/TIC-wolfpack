## About Affordable Driving School

Affordable Driving School has been a trusted name in Queensland driver education for decades. Serving Brisbane, Gold Coast, and surrounding suburbs, Affordable is built on the principle that high-quality driving instruction should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget.

With friendly, experienced instructors and competitive pricing, Affordable removes the financial barriers that can delay young Queenslanders from getting on the road. Every lesson delivers the same professional standard of instruction, with no compromise in quality at the lower price point.

As part of The Instructor College group, Affordable Driving School benefits from shared systems, admin support, and consistent quality standards across the group, while keeping the approachable, value-focused identity that has made it a household name across South East Queensland.
