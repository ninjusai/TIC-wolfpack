## About Affordable Driving School

Affordable Driving School has been a trusted name in Queensland driver training for decades. Serving Brisbane, Gold Coast, and surrounding suburbs, Affordable is built on the principle that high-quality driving instruction should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget.

With friendly, experienced instructors and competitive pricing, Affordable removes the financial barriers that can delay young Queenslanders from getting on the road. Every lesson delivers the same professional standard of instruction — the only difference is the price tag.

As part of The Instructor College group, Affordable Driving School benefits from group-wide training standards and operational support while maintaining the approachable, value-focused identity that has made it a household name across South East Queensland.
