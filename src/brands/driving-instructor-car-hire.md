## About Driving Instructor Car Hire

Driving Instructor Car Hire is a specialist B2B vehicle hire service for accredited driving instructors, operating across Brisbane's Northside and Southside. It is not a learner-driver hire service. Customers are professional instructors and driving school operators who need access to a dual-control vehicle to keep teaching.

When an instructor's own car is off the road for servicing, repairs, accident replacement, or any other reason, Driving Instructor Car Hire provides a maintained dual-control vehicle on short-term or ongoing hire. The service is designed for business continuity: it keeps instructors earning, keeps lessons running, and keeps driving schools delivering on bookings.

As an industry service within The Instructor College group, Driving Instructor Car Hire supports the wider driver training industry rather than competing with the group's driving school brands. The audience is professional, the offering is B2B, and the purpose is practical: a fleet that helps accredited instructors keep working when their own vehicle cannot.
