## About Driving Instructor Car Hire

Driving Instructor Car Hire is the TIC group's dedicated B2B service brand, providing dual-control replacement vehicles to driving instructors and driving schools across Brisbane's Northside and Southside. When an instructor's vehicle is off the road for servicing, repairs, or any other reason, Driving Instructor Car Hire ensures they can keep teaching without interruption.

Unlike the group's consumer-facing driving school brands, Driving Instructor Car Hire serves the driver training industry itself. The fleet of dual-control vehicles is maintained to a high standard and available for short-term and ongoing hire, giving instructors the flexibility they need to keep their businesses running.

As a specialist service within The Instructor College group, Driving Instructor Car Hire fills a critical gap in the Queensland driver training ecosystem — keeping instructors on the road and students in lessons.
