## About RAC School of Motoring

RAC School of Motoring operates with a clear mission: to reduce young driver fatalities on Queensland roads. Based in Brisbane and the Sunshine Coast, RAC is committed to producing capable, confident, and safe drivers through top-quality instruction.

Every lesson at RAC is designed with road safety at its core. From test preparation to ongoing skill development, instructors focus on building the judgment and awareness that keep new drivers safe in real traffic conditions. This safety-driven ethos sets RAC apart: it is more than a driving school, it is a road safety advocate.

As part of The Instructor College group, RAC School of Motoring shares in a network-wide commitment to instructor excellence and student outcomes that prioritise long-term safety over short-term pass rates.
